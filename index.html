Models

  • Logit model
    • Binary explanatory variables
  • Dependent variable: 1 if purchase was made during visit, 0 otherwise
  • Three categories of factors:
    • Location
    • Marketing
    • User
  • Only signs from coefficients can be interpreted

Methodology:

  • For each category, choose factors that have at least 10 sales

Conclusions

  • North American visitors in the sample were more likely to make a purchase
    • Otherwise, location results marginally significant
  • People who visited from Google campus more likely to purchase
  • Visitors from youtube less likely to make a purchase
  • People in Google's ecosystem (Chrome OS, Chrome browser) more likely to purchase
